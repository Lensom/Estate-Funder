About Estate Funder

Estate Funder allows investors to collectively invest small amounts of capital into premium real estate projects and earn above average returns plus share in project profits.

Estate Funder makes it possible for people on any budget to start investing in multimillion dollar projects that were previously only accessible to weathy investors and institutions.

We source, evaluate and provide initial funding for the real estate are secured for our investments which are listed on our platform. This process is managed by our team who have over 75 years of combined experience in real estate investing.

We believe that by being an active financial participant in all investments, underscores our commitment and confidence in all offering listed on the platform. For investors, the entire investment process is undertaken and managed through the Estate Funder online account dashboard. All Investments made through the Estate Funder platform are secured over real estate.
