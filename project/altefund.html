Investment strategy

We acquire, develop and create value in real estate assets to maximise your
investment return.

1Acquire Real Estate

We aim to acquire real estate in strategic locations and at below market or competitive rates with the aim of maximising the overall returns on your investment.

2Develop the real estate

We develop the real estate to create value.. The research that is undertaken as part of our acquistion process allows us to identify and aquire sites with favourable planning controls resulting in streamlined development programs.

3Exit & Profit

Once development of the real estate is complete, we sell down the individual units to realize the value that has been created through our acquisition and development programs. The returns are then distributed to our investors.
